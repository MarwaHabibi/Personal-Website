Common Questions

How can I contact Marwa Habibi?

You can contact me via the Contact page on this website or by sending an email to marwahabibi789@gmail.com.

What topics does Marwa write about?

My writing focuses primarily on education, women's rights, peacebuilding, and social issues in Afghanistan, along with personal stories and literary works.

Can I contribute guest posts to the blog?

Currently, I do not accept guest posts. However, you are welcome to reach out if you have ideas or collaboration proposals.

Are the blog posts available in other languages?

Most posts are in English, but I am working on providing some content in Persian (Farsi) and other languages in the future.

Where can I find Marwa’s published books or articles?

Links to published works will be available in the Article section or through direct announcements on the blog.
